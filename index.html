About

All of the coffees we select meet our standards for quality and sustainability.

Our coffees are grown at least 1100 meters above sea level and higher are considered Strictly High Grown in Honduras. The slower growth at the higher elevations produces slower ripening process which in turn produces a sweeter coffee bean.
